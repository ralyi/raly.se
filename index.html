About

Like you, a life in progress

I grew up in Giza and studied Computer Science and Engineering at the German University in Cairo, graduating in 2014. This gave me a technical foundation that I didn't fully appreciate at the time.

While studying, I got involved with AIESEC and spent a good chunk of my early twenties facilitating exchange programmes and travelling across the world. That period genuinely changed how I see the world. One of those exchanges brought me to Stockholm for an internship, and somehow, ten years later, I'm still here. I have a family, a mortgage, and a favourite fika spot. I'm grateful to be able to call Stockholm home.

Professionally, I've moved between engineering, product, and sales roles across industries. The thread running through all of it is the same: I like being in the room where technical complexity meets human decisions, and helping the two sides understand each other.
